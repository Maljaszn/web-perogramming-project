Timer Help Page

Getting Started

To set the timer, simply click on the "Start" button and select the desired time interval.

Using the Timer

To stop the timer, click on the "Stop" button. To reset the timer, click on the "Reset" button.

Additional Features

Our timer comes with an alarm feature that will alert you when the countdown is complete.

Troubleshooting

If the timer is not working, try refreshing the page or checking your browser settings.
